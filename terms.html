Terms & Conditions

Please review our rules regarding tickets, boardings, schedules, and luggage policies.

1. Ticket Booking & Validity

All tickets reserved online, offline, or via our WhatsApp API are valid only for the specific seat details, travel dates, routes, and boarding slots selected. Bookings are non-transferable and require a valid photo identity card during passenger check-in verification processes.

2. Boarding & Punctuality

Passengers are required to report at the specified pickup point at least 15 minutes before the scheduled boarding window. Shree Balaji Bus Travels is not responsible for missing buses if passengers fail to report on time. Buses will not delay departures for late arrivals.

3. Luggage Policies & Restrictions

While we provide secure carriage in the luggage hold, carrying valuable items (laptops, jewelry, cash documents) in the hold is done at the owner's risk. Shree Balaji Bus Travels is not liable for minor wears, damages, or losses. Carrying inflammable materials, explosives, weapons, or contraband items is strictly prohibited.

4. Route Cancellations & Bus Changes

In case of mechanical breakdowns, highway blockages, or natural disasters, Shree Balaji Bus Travels reserves the right to reschedule departure timings, change the type of coach bus allocated, or cancel operations. In case of full cancellations, a 100% ticket refund will be issued.
