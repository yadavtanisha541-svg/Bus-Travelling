Who We Are

Shree Balaji Bus Travels

Established in 2011, Shree Balaji Bus Travels has grown into one of the country's most trustworthy passenger transport providers. We specialize in connecting major tourist destinations and financial hubs with modern premium sleeper coaches.

Our goal is to make every bus journey feel like a flight. From GPS location tracking and active security surveillance to comfortable pushback sleeper berths and clean linen, we constantly implement innovations to make your travels cozy and safe.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified
Ministry of Tourism Approved

Our Mission

To provide passengers with top-tier travel experiences focused on safety, unmatched comfort, schedule punctuality, and friendly support services at the best possible price values.

Our Vision

To expand our networks across every corner of India, establishing standard-setting smart fleets, setting bench marks for high-end luxury, and transforming highway travel standards.

Milestones

Our Growth History

How we expanded from a single minibus to a nation-wide brand operator.

2011 - The Origin

Started operations with only two minibuses, conducting corporate trips and school tour bookings locally.

2015 - Volvo Fleet Addition

Added our first premium Volvo multi-axle sleeper buses, launching regular intercity passenger routes.

2019 - Nationwide Tours

Launched all-inclusive domestic tour packages, booking luxury pilgrimage and holiday routes across northern states.

2026 - Smart Operations

Empowered fleets with high-speed Wi-Fi, GPS tracking, online ticketing interfaces, and safe travel validations.
