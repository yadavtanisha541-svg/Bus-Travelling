Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant support and answers regarding refunds, routes, boarding, and bus bookings.

How do I book tickets online with Shree Balaji Travels?
You can navigate to our Booking Page, choose your pickup/drop points, travel date, select seats interactively, fill passenger details, and hit submit. You can also click the "WhatsApp Booking" button to directly send a pre-filled reservation format to our ticketing desk.
What is the cancellation and refund policy?
Cancellations made 24 hours prior to travel time receive a 90% refund. Cancellations made between 12-24 hours prior receive a 50% refund. No refunds are issued for cancellations done within 12 hours of boarding. Refunds are processed back to the origin payment method within 5-7 business days.
What is the allowed luggage limit per passenger?
Each ticket holder is allowed to carry up to 20kg of personal luggage free of charge in the bus luggage hold. Small handbags and laptop bags are allowed inside passenger cabins. Excess luggage is subject to additional weight rates depending on the route coordinates.
Do your buses provide charging points and Wi-Fi?
Yes! All our premium Volvo, Scania, and Maharaja AC Sleeper coaches are equipped with high-speed passenger Wi-Fi routers and individual USB charging slots next to each seat or sleeping berth.
Can I rent an entire coach bus for weddings or corporate events?
Absolutely. We run specialized services for wedding transports, employee shuttles, corporate tours, and school day outings. Please check our Services Page or submit a custom quote request via the contact form on our contact page.
